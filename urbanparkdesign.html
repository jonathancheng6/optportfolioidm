Urban Park Design

High School for Math, Science, & Engineering Neptali Castro

The Urban Park Design Project was a concept design for the current parking lot of City College of New York. The goal of the design was to define spaces with earth's shapes and trees. The new space was to be dedicated for students to relax from thier busy school schedules.

My final design incorporated a small bridge for elevation, running water for white noise, and a slight downward slope in the back for isolation.
