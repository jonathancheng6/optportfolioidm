Jonathan Cheng

Cube House

weekend retreat house

Jonathan Cheng

ABOUT

An design project that defines spaces using simple planes and voids. The project followed a program for a weekend retreat house; consisitng of a private sleeping area, storage area, and a public area for guests. The house is 20'x 20'x 20' and the plot of land is 80' x 80'. The use of hedges created implied spaces on the property. I split the plot of land down the middle to separate the public space and private space; I used the water feature, which connected to the exisiting lake, to emphasize this separation.
